Who I Am

I'm a Cybersecurity graduate from Visvesvaraya Technological University with a deep specialisation in Blue Team operations. My work covers the full SOC lifecycle — from alert triage and SIEM configuration to proactive threat hunting and hands-on forensic investigation.

I understand attacker behaviour at the TTPs level. Using MITRE ATT&CK to drive detection logic, I've simulated full C2 attack chains, hunted fileless malware artifacts in Windows Registry, and analyzed system behavior to bridge the gap between exploitation and defense.
